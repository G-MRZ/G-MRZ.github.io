## ubuntu hugo安装

[https://github.com/gohugoio/hugo/releases](https://github.com/gohugoio/hugo/releases)下载hugo*.dep安装包

```在安装包存放目录使用sudo dpkg -i hugo*.dep进行安装```

## 命令

```hugo new site [bolg name] 生成博客```

```hugo new blog/xx.md 生成文章```

```hugo server 启动hugo```

```hugo 构建博客```
